Self Test

To check the probability of having Anemia just solve a small quiz given below

1)Do you often experience fatigue and weakness?(Fatigue has many causes besides anemia,so don't assume that you're tired then you must be anemic.Some people learn that their haemoglobin is low,which indicates anemia)

2)Has the colour of your skin turned pale or yellowish ?

3)Do you have irregular heartbeats ?

4)Do you often experience shortness or difficulty in breathing ?

5)Do you feel dizzy or light headed ?

6)Do you experience chest pain ?

7)Are your hands and feet cold ?

8)Do you experience headaches ?

Note*:The results that you will receive are strictly based on the answers selected by you and results are not 100% accurate . If you receive results that suggest you have Anemia , we would request you to not panic and consult a doctor before starting any medications .
